~ Bella ~

Bella is 3 years old girl from France. She is very friendly and playful.
She was adopted in 2018 from VOKRA. Initially, she was a shy girl, so she hide herself behind the curtain.
Eventually, she has started to trust human again, and now she is one of the main cat in our cat cafe!
From 3 pm to 4pm, she usually sleep on her favorite couch located on the right corner of our shop.
During her sleeping time, please do not disturb her.

Her favorite things: eating fish,playing with cat toy, sleeping, welcoming people by cooing.
