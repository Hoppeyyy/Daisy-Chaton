Our Home-Made Coffee Beans

Our company provides organic home-made coffee beans. With no added synthetic fertilizers and/or chemicals, we natural grow our beans through a plant-based farm. Our coffee beans are harvested from trees and are 100% produced in the lower mainland. produced in Britich Columbia. As there are 4 different types of coffee beans, Daisy Chaton provides natural Arabica and Liberica coffee beans.

Growning our own plant-based coffee beans began with our idea in supporting local rescuse cats. With each purchase of coffee that is made with our Arabica and Liberica beans we will be donating money Vokra, an organization and centre for rescue cats.
